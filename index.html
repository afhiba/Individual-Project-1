About Me

My name is Atia Hiba. I was born in Kuwait and moved to the US when I was 8 months old. I can speak English and Bengali fluently and Spanish at an intermediete level. As the middle child among four sisters, life has always been lively. Outside of school and work, I enjoy working out, boxing, spending time with family and friends, going on spontaneous adventures, and travelling. I also love to journal, using writing as a way to reflect. My interest in engineering and technology started at a young age, which ultimately led me to pursue a degree in Information Technology.

Favorite Sports

  • Soccer: This sport holds a special place in my heart, as it’s a family tradition. The excitement during the FIFA World Cup season and major league games, where we all come together makes for unforgettable moments.
  • Basketball: I enjoy playing basketball not just for the thrill of the game, but also for the opportunity it provides to connect with friends and stay active. It’s a fun way to bond while enjoying some friendly competition.
  • Badminton: Having played badminton since I was a kid, this sport has always been a part of my life. I love the intensity and competitiveness it brings.
